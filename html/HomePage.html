HIGHER STUDIES TRAINING

Many students plan to pursue higher studies after completing their undergraduation. If you are one among them, you have come to the right place. We are offering coaching to students to prepare for the various top competitive exams, to get into top universities in India and abroad. Students can register for their desired course by filling in the form in the 'Higher Studies' section. The students will be coached by the seniors pursuing Post Graduation in BMS College Of Engineering. Students will be familiarised with the pattern of the respective exams, and will be trained accordingly. There will be regular tests conducted to hone the writing, speaking and listening skills, which form a major part of these exams.

NOTIFICATIONS:
  • Upcoming Tests: GRE,GATE speaking skills test...
  • Change in timings for Interview training...
  • Results of Test 1 and Test 2 for TOEFL and CAT students.
  • Upcoming general questionnaire session.
  • Timetable for aptitude tests.

PLACEMENT TRAINING

Planning to sit for Placements instead? You have come to the right place too. We are offering training to help students gain knowlegde on the various procedures involved in placements. Mock aptitude and skill tests, interview practices and awareness of soft skills will be a part of this training, along with topic specific coaching. Final year and PG students of BMS College of Engineering will be the instructors. Student can register for specific courses under the 'Placement Section'. After undergoing this training, students will gain the confidence and right skills required to sit for placements.
